Hot Chocolate

Home Page

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk (whole milk for creaminess, or any milk of choice)
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Pinch of salt (to enhance the chocolate flavor)
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional toppings)
  • Chopped dark or milk chocolate (optional, for a richer version)

Steps

  1. Add Milk
    • Gradually add 2 cups of milk while whisking to dissolve the dry ingredients smoothly.
  2. Heat the Mixture
    • Place the saucepan over medium heat.
    • Stir constantly until the mixture is hot but not boiling (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add Flavor (Optional)
    • Stir in ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract for extra depth of flavor.
  4. Serve
    • Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream, marshmallows, or a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon.
